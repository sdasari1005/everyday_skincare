Our Story

Born in India,
For India

Everyday Skincare was founded with one simple belief: skincare should be honest, effective, and made for real Indian skin — not a Western formula repurposed for our diverse melanin-rich complexions.

Our team of Indian dermatologists and cosmetic chemists spent years researching the unique needs of Indian skin — from the humidity of Mumbai to the dry air of Delhi, from dusky South Indian tones to fair North Indian complexions.

Every formula starts with ancient Indian botanical wisdom — turmeric, saffron, neem, coconut — enhanced with modern active ingredients, giving you the best of both worlds.
